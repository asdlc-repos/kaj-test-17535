# Overview

An internal application where WSO2 employees request time off and managers approve or reject those requests.

# Personas

- Employee — requests leave and checks their available balance.
- Manager — reviews and responds to leave requests from their team.

# Features

- An employee submits a leave request for specific dates with a reason.
- Managers see pending requests from their direct reports.
- A manager approves or rejects a request with optional comments.
- Employees view their remaining leave balance by type.
- Everyone sees the status of their own requests.
